About the Series

Three heartbroken friends go on a vacation to forget their past. Little do they know, their charming tour guide, Levi, is actually a paid actor hired to make their trip more "romantic" for social media clout.

As feelings grow messy between the friends and their guide, the biggest question remains: Was any of it real? Or was it all just part of Tita Bebang's scheme to make her inn go viral?

This romantic comedy web series explores modern relationships, social media facades, and the blurred lines between performance and reality.
